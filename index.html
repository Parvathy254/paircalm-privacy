Privacy Policy for PairCalm

Last Updated: March 2, 2026

PairCalm ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data within the PairCalm mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: We collect your name and email address via Firebase Authentication.
  • Household Data: We store chores, durations, and "Burst" messages to sync across devices.
  • Audio Data: Voice recordings are processed for transcription and are not stored permanently.

2. Use of Artificial Intelligence

PairCalm uses the Google Gemini API to provide "Coach Insights." We do not use your personal prompts to train AI models.

3. Data Deletion

You can delete your account and data via the Settings menu or by contacting hello.paircalm@gmail.com.
