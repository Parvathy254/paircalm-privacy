Privacy Policy for PairCalm

Last Updated: March 2, 2026

PairCalm ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data within the PairCalm mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: We collect your name and email address via Firebase Authentication.
  • Household Data: We store chores, task durations, and "Burst" messages to sync them within your household.
  • Audio Data: Voice recordings are processed ephemerally for transcription and are not stored permanently.
  • Device Identifiers: We collect non-identifying IDs for app functionality and security via Firebase.

2. Use of Artificial Intelligence

PairCalm uses the Google Gemini API to provide "Coach Insights" and features.

  • Privacy: We do not use your personal prompts to train AI models.

3. Data Storage & Sharing

Data is stored securely using Google Firebase and is shared only with your linked partner. We do not sell data to third parties.

4. Data Deletion & Management

  • Delete All Data: You can wipe all chores and history via the Settings menu while keeping your account.
  • Account Deletion: You can delete your entire account and all associated data via the Settings menu.

5. Important Disclaimers

Educational Tool Only: PairCalm and its AI-generated "Coach Insights" are intended solely for informational and organizational purposes. PairCalm is not a medical device, a mental health therapy tool, or a substitute for professional medical, psychological, or legal advice. Always seek the advice of a qualified professional for any health or legal concerns.
