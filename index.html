Privacy Policy for PairCalm

Last Updated: March 2, 2026

PairCalm ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data within the PairCalm mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: We collect your name and email address via Firebase Authentication to create your account and link you with your partner.
  • Household Data: We store chores, task durations, and "Burst" messages to sync them across devices in your household.
  • Audio Data: We process voice recordings only when you use the Voice Log feature. This audio is used solely for speech-to-text transcription and is not stored permanently as an audio file on our servers.

2. Use of Artificial Intelligence

PairCalm uses the Google Gemini API to provide "Coach Insights" and tone-shifting features.

  • Data Processing: Recent tasks and messages are sent to the API to generate relationship insights.
  • Privacy: We do not use your personal prompts to train AI models. Processing is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

3. Data Storage & Sharing

  • Storage: Data is stored securely using Google Firebase.
  • Sharing: Your data is shared only with the specific partner you have linked to your Household ID. We do not sell or share your data with third-party advertisers.

4. Data Deletion

  • In-App: You can delete your account and all associated data at any time via the Settings menu.
  • External Request: To request data deletion if you no longer have the app installed, please contact us at hello.paircalm@gmail.com.

5. Contact

For any questions, please contact us at hello.paircalm@gmail.com.
