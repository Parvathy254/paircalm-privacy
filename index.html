Privacy Policy for PairCalm

Effective Date: March 2, 2026

PairCalm ("we," "our," or "us") provides a collaborative household management tool. We are committed to transparency regarding how your data is collected, processed, and protected.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Data: Name and email address collected via Firebase Authentication to identify users and link household partners.
  • Household Content: Chores, task durations, and "Burst" messages created by users to synchronize data between linked devices.
  • Audio Inputs: Voice recordings are captured only upon active user initiation for the Voice Log feature. This audio is processed ephemerally for transcription and is not stored as a permanent audio file on our servers.
  • Technical Identifiers: We collect non-identifying Firebase Installation IDs to facilitate secure data syncing and push notifications.

2. Use of Artificial Intelligence

PairCalm utilizes the Google Gemini API to provide "Coach Insights" and tone-shifting features.

  • Data Processing: To generate relevant relationship insights, the app transmits recent task descriptions and message text to the Gemini API.
  • Privacy & Training: Per our enterprise agreement, your personal prompts and data are not used to train Google’s underlying AI models. AI processing is conducted in accordance with Google’s Generative AI Data Processing Terms.

3. Data Storage & Sharing

  • Secure Storage: All user data is stored using Google Firebase, utilizing industry-standard encryption both in transit (TLS) and at rest.
  • Restricted Sharing: Your data is shared only with the specific partner linked to your unique Household ID.
  • No Third-Party Sales: We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third-party advertisers or data brokers.

4. Data Deletion & Management

  • Delete All Data (Partial Deletion): Users may wipe all household content (chores, history, and preferences) without closing their account via the "Delete All Data" button in Settings.
  • Full Account Deletion: Users may permanently delete their account and all associated personal data via the Settings menu or by contacting support.
