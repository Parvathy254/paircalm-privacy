Privacy Policy for PairCalm

Last Updated: March 2, 2026

PairCalm ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data within the PairCalm mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: We collect your name and email address via Firebase Authentication to create your account and link you with your partner.
  • Household Data: We store chores, task durations, and "Burst" messages to sync them across devices in your household.
  • Audio Data: We process voice recordings only when you use the Voice Log feature. This audio is processed ephemerally for transcription and is not stored permanently on our servers.
  • Device Identifiers: We collect non-identifying device IDs for app functionality, security, and push notifications via Firebase.

2. Use of Artificial Intelligence

PairCalm uses the Google Gemini API to provide "Coach Insights" and tone-shifting features.

  • Data Processing: Relevant task data and messages are sent to the API to generate insights.
  • Privacy: We do not use your personal prompts to train AI models. Processing is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

3. Data Storage & Sharing

  • Storage: Data is stored securely using Google Firebase (encrypted in transit and at rest).
  • Sharing: Your data is shared only with your linked partner. We do not sell or share your data with third-party advertisers or unauthorized organizations.

4. Data Deletion & Management

We provide users with full control over their data in compliance with Google Play requirements:

  • Delete All Data (Partial Deletion): You can wipe all chores, history, and preferences while keeping your account active via the "Delete All Data" button in the App Settings.
  • Account Deletion: You can delete your entire account and all associated personal data via the Settings menu.
  • External Requests: If you no longer have the app installed, you may request account or data deletion by contacting us at the email below. We process all requests within 48 hours.
