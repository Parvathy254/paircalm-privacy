Privacy Policy for PairCalm

Last Updated: March 2, 2026

PairCalm ("we," "our," or "us") provides a collaborative household management tool. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how your data is handled.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Data: Name and email address collected via Firebase Authentication to identify users and link household partners.
  • Household Content: Chores, task durations, and "Burst" messages created by users to synchronize data between linked devices.
  • Audio Inputs: Voice recordings are processed only when you use the Voice Log feature. This audio is used solely for speech-to-text transcription and is not stored permanently as an audio file on our servers.
  • Technical Identifiers: We collect non-identifying Firebase Installation IDs to facilitate secure data syncing and push notifications.

2. Use of Artificial Intelligence

PairCalm uses the Google Gemini API to provide "Coach Insights" and tone-shifting features.

  • Data Processing: Recent tasks and messages are sent to the API to generate relationship insights.
  • Privacy: We do not use your personal prompts to train AI models. Processing is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

3. Data Storage & Sharing

  • Storage: Data is stored securely using Google Firebase.
  • Sharing: Your data is shared only with the specific partner you have linked to your Household ID. We do not sell or share your data with third-party advertisers.

4. Data Deletion & Management

  • Delete All Data (Partial Deletion): Users may wipe all household content (chores, history, and preferences) without closing their account via the "Delete All Data" button in Settings.
  • Full Account Deletion: Users may permanently delete their account and all associated personal data via the Settings menu.
  • External Request: To request data deletion if you no longer have the app installed, please contact us at hello.paircalm@gmail.com.
IMPORTANT DISCLAIMER: PairCalm is an organizational tool and educational AI resource. It does not substitute for professional medical, psychological, or legal requirements. The "Coach Insights" are AI-generated suggestions and should not be treated as professional medical, mental health, or legal advice.
